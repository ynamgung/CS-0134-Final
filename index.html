HELLO

Rabbit cafes are unique adoption platforms, allowing potential adopters to interact with the rabbits prior to making a decision to adopt. Traditionally, rabbits are held in cages, which allow for little interaction between the two, and can inhibit adoption rates. The socialization with people further rehabilitates our rabbits and get them ready for a forever home. Many people who love rabbits and crave their company visit the cafe to get their rabbit fix if they are unable to own, are allergic, or otherwise. You can also study here as well: we have tables, chairs, and couches. Did we mention free WiFi?
