Where are you located?

Our cafe is located on 40 Meadow Street, in the Chelsea neighborhood of Manhattan, in New York City. Other attractions around our cafe include Chelsea Market, the High Line, and the Rubin Museum of Art.

Where are the rabbits from?

Our rabbits are from the local ASPCA. As more rabbits became abandoned at shelters, the demands became impossible to fulfill so we decided to help out by taking them in.

Can I adopt the rabbits?

Yes, all of the rabbits in our cafe are up for adoption. Please fill out the adoption form on our site if you are interested and we will get back to you shortly.
